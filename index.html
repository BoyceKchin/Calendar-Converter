CSV → ICS Converter

Instructions

  1. Save your Excel file as a CSV file.
  2. Check that the Date column uses MM/DD/YYYY format.
  3. Ensure the Time column uses a format like 9 AM - 5 PM.
  4. Click Select CSV File and choose your file.
  5. Click Convert to generate an .ics calendar file.
  6. Once complete, click the Download ICS link to save your calendar.
